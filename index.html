Who We Are

Two College Students
With a Simple Idea.

We're Nathan Stezzi and Dylan Torres — two seniors at Loyola University Maryland. We're not computer scientists or engineers. We're just two people who figured out how to use AI tools to build really good websites for small businesses.

We started T.S. Pro because we saw a gap: local business owners needed websites but couldn't justify paying thousands to an agency, and the cheap template builders all looked the same.

AI changed the game. We can now build a professional, custom-looking website in a fraction of the time it used to take — and pass those savings on to you. You work with us directly, start to finish, no middleman.
